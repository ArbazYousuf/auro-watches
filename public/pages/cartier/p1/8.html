MODEL CASE

The Speedmaster 38 mm collection has been crafted with simplicity, size and colour in mind, yet still retains the look and heritage of OMEGA's most famous Chronograph.


This Model features a stainless steel and 18K Sedna TMgold case with a dual bezel design. The inner section of the bezzel features a tachymeter scale on a brown aluminium ring while the outside is diamond-paved.


The "Cappuccino" dail includes horizontal brown oval subdails and a vertical oval date window at 6 o'clock. The indexes and hand are made up of 18K SednaTM gold with white Super-LumiNova on the hour, minute and chronograph seconds hands.


The Watch has a taupe-brown leather strap, a seahorse medallion on the caseback, and is driven by the OMEGA Co-Axial Calibre 3330.
